Palorant x Arcane: A Legendary Crossover Begins!

UPDATE GAME

10/1/2025

A thrilling collaboration has just landed — Palorant teams up with the world of Arcane in a limited-time event packed with stunning visuals, exclusive cosmetics, and subtle lore tie-ins. Whether you're a fan of Piltover tech or Zaun's chaos, there's something for every agent this patch!

Arcane Re:Edition Bundle Now Available

Celebrate the crossover with a stylish new bundle featuring:

  • Vi’s Gauntlet Knife – A heavy-hitting melee with smash animation effects.
  • Hextech Phantom – Transparent weapon skin with electric particle trails.
  • Zaunite Spray Pack – Unique graffiti sprays inspired by Arcane's street art.

New Voice Lines & Environmental Touches

Select agents—such as Naon and Jetti—will now deliver Arcane-inspired voice lines during key moments in combat.

Meanwhile, maps Rupture and Tolus receive temporary thematic updates:

  • Steampunk murals featuring Arcane characters.
  • Modified lighting and skybox for a more "Piltover vs. Zaun" vibe.
  • Hidden Hexbug drones lurking in corners for players to find.

Minor Patch 1.2 Updates

  • Brimdirt: Mudtrap cooldown reduced from 12s to 10s.
  • Jetti: Pulse Overdrive animation now smoother on lower-end systems.
  • Fixed a bug where Cucumber's utility would hover mid-air on Spit.
  • Adjusted Acsent attacker spawn point for fairer early engagements.

Event Details

  • Crossover event duration: April 17 – May 7, 2025
  • Log in during the event to claim the exclusive Arcane Player Card for free!

Developer's Note

“Arcane’s world is rich in narrative and visual identity—it fits so well into the intensity of Palorant. We’re incredibly excited to bring this universe into ours, even if just for a moment.”

— Palorant Creative Director
