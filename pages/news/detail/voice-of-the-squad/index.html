Voices of the Squad: Palorant Community Celebration

COMMUNITY

7/3/2025

The heart of Palorant has always been its community. From clutch montages and fan art masterpieces to grassroots tournaments and cosplay showcases, our players constantly surprise us with their talent, passion, and creativity. With this Community Celebration update, we're shining a spotlight on YOU—the agents behind the screen.

New Community Vibes

This patch introduces several features requested directly by players through forums, social media, and in-game feedback. We’ve listened, and here’s what’s coming:

  • Custom Voice Packs: Equip limited voice lines recorded by top community creators and streamers. Hear your favorite lines in action!

  • Celebration Lobby Themes: Choose a background inspired by community-favorite maps, fan art, or your top agents.

  • Top Clip Integration: Players with viral highlights may be featured on the login screen weekly.

Community-Created Cosmetic Showcase

We’ve collaborated with several Palorant artists to bring their dreams to life in-game. Through a curated selection process, we’ve turned some of the best community submissions into official items:

  • “Agent Naon: Midnight Ops” Player Card – Based on fan illustration by @KireiPixels.

  • “Ace or Nothing” Spray – Graffiti-styled spray submitted by player SmudgeFX.

  • Community Bundle: Heartline – Weapon skins co-designed with input from over 100 players during a month-long voting event.

Spotlight on Creators

This month’s Community Spotlight highlights three standout creators who’ve redefined what it means to be part of Palorant:

  • Zephy: For their insane montage editing and weekly "Agent Breakdown" series that helps new players improve fast.

  • Artemii: A cosplayer from Ukraine who recreated Ohmenn’s entire look using recycled materials—pure dedication!

  • The Spitfire League: A community-run tournament for underdog players that has now featured over 500 participants.

Event Details

  • Event Duration: May 17 – June 10, 2025

  • Login Bonus: Receive a free “Squad United” Player Title for logging in during the celebration.

  • Creative Challenge: Submit your fan art, story, or highlight clip for a chance to be officially featured!

Developer's Note

“Palorant wouldn’t be what it is without its players. Every time we see a new highlight, meme, cosplay, or breakdown video—we’re reminded why we build this game. This patch is our thank-you. Let’s keep building, laughing, fragging, and growing—together.”

— The Palorant Community Team
