Palorant x Naruto: Shadow Strikes Crossover Patch

COLLABORATION

17/5/2025

A legendary crossover arrives as Palorant teams up with Naruto in a limited-time event that blends the shinobi world with tactical combat. Expect thematic cosmetics, animated finishers, and a whole new vibe across multiple maps. Whether you're from the Hidden Leaf or just love stylish moves, this update brings high-energy action to your matches.

Patch 1.4 Highlights

The ninja spirit is alive with new gameplay effects and UI overlays.

  • Shadow Clone Decoys added as visual illusions during certain agent abilities.

  • New UI reticle options inspired by Sharingan and Byakugan designs.

  • Agents now leave behind chakra trails while sprinting, visible to teammates only.

Map Additions

Three maps—Coldbox, Spit, and Acsent—receive visual modifications inspired by Naruto’s world.

Expect a more immersive ninja battlefield.

  • Coldbox features a Hidden Leaf Village overlay in spawn areas.

  • Spit adds the Valley of the End statues near the central zone.

  • Acsent shows off Akatsuki-themed skyboxes during the final rounds.

Ninja Arsenal Bundle

  • Kusanagi Blade (Melee) – A glowing sword skin with a special dash animation.

  • Kurama Phantom – A fiery orange weapon skin with nine-tail traces during kills.

  • Summoning Jutsu Spray – Animated spray of Gamabunta leaping in.

Event Details

  • Event Duration: May 17 – June 6, 2025

  • Earn exclusive titles like "Hokage in Training" and "Shadow Operative" through weekly missions.

  • Players who log in during the event will receive a free Naruto Player Card.

Developer's Note

“As long-time anime fans, working with Naruto’s universe was a dream come true. From jutsus to clan rivalry, we wanted to pay tribute to its core themes while still keeping Palorant’s style intact.”

— Palorant Live Events Director
