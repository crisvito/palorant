Palorant Patch 1 . 2 – Rise of Stalber

UPDATE GAME

1/5/2025

Patch 1.2 introduces the long-awaited Stalber rework, an all-new skin collection, and several critical map updates. This patch aims to push Palorant into a new strategic era, where bold plays and adaptability are more rewarding than ever.

Patch 1.3 Highlights

The Stalber Rework brings fresh mechanics and balance changes:

  • Adrenaline Surge (Passive) now activates at 20% HP, giving a burst of movement speed and regen.
  • EMP Toss radius increased by 15%, with better visual clarity on activation.
  • Added new voice lines when Stalber wins a 1v3 situation.

Map Overhauls

With this patch, Coldbox and Rupture undergo substantial layout revisions. These updates are designed to improve rotation clarity, attacker entry points, and overall pacing in competitive matches.

Changes include:

  • Removed double crates in Coldbox B-site for more open duels.
  • Rupture mid restructured to offer better crossfire and flanking options.
  • New weather effects added dynamically on Coldbox during late rounds.

New Skin Collection: Carbon Drift

  • Carbon Drift Vandal – Matte black with pulsing teal lines.
  • Stalber's Claw (Melee) – Custom animation when equipping after a kill.
  • Reactive Spray – Changes color based on your KDA in the match.

Event Details

  • Event Duration: May 1 – May 21, 2025
  • Complete in-game missions with Stalber to unlock exclusive voice lines and a player title.

Developer's Note

“This patch is a huge milestone for us. With Stalber finally receiving the attention he deserves, and our ongoing commitment to map clarity, we hope players will find new strategies to explore.”

— Palorant Systems Design Team
